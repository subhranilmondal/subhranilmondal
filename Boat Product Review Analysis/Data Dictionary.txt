
BoatProduct.csv: This file contains the product information of boat company such as ProductName, ProductPrice, Discount, NumberofReviews, Rate, and Review and Summary.

Columns:
ProductName: Name of the product.
ProductPrice: Price of the product.
Discount: Discount of the product.
NumberofReviews: number of review by customer
Rate: Customer rating for the product .
Review: Customer's Review of the product.
Summary: This file contains the detailed descriptions of each product such as product specifications, features, and product dimensions.
